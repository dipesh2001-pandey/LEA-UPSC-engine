# Sprint 2 Design Journal

**Sprint:** Sprint 2 – Enterprise Dynamics

---

# Objective

Design the dynamic architecture of the Learning Enterprise Architecture (LEA).

---

# Major Discoveries

## Enterprise Engines

Capabilities own responsibility.

Enterprise Engines own behaviour.

---

## Knowledge Engineering

Knowledge Assets are not inputs.

They are engineered outputs.

Information is transformed into reusable Knowledge Assets organised within Themes.

---

## Learning

Learning is not downstream of Knowledge Engineering.

Learning occurs simultaneously with Knowledge Engineering.

---

## Cognitive Core

Knowledge Engineering Engine

⇄

Learning Engine

Together they form the Cognitive Core of LEA.

---

## Assessment

Assessment measures learning.

It does not create learning.

Its purpose is to generate Assessment Evidence and Performance Feedback.

---

## Governance

Governance continuously analyses enterprise feedback and evolves the architecture.

The enterprise itself becomes a learning system.

---

## Enterprise Value Stream

Information

↓

Knowledge Engineering

↓

Knowledge Assets

↓

Learning

↓

Learning State

↓

Assessment

↓

Assessment Evidence

↓

Governance

↓

Enterprise Improvements

↓

Knowledge Engineering

---

# What Changed?

Sprint 1 described **what LEA is**.

Sprint 2 describes **how LEA behaves**.

The architecture evolved from a static capability model into a closed adaptive enterprise.

---
