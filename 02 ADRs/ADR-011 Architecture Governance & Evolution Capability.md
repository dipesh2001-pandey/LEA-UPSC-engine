# ADR-011: Architecture Governance & Evolution Capability

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-06-20

---

# Context

LEA is intended to evolve continuously through implementation feedback.

Architectural decisions must remain traceable, reviewable and adaptable without compromising enterprise integrity.

A dedicated governance capability is required to ensure controlled evolution.

---

# Decision

Architecture Governance & Evolution is the enterprise capability responsible for governing the evolution of LEA through architectural reviews, ADRs, implementation feedback and continuous improvement.

This capability owns the **Architecture State** of LEA.

---

# Purpose

To ensure that LEA evolves systematically while preserving architectural integrity.

---

# Scope

Architecture Governance & Evolution is responsible for:

- Architecture Reviews
- Architectural Decision Records (ADRs)
- Design Journal
- Capability Evolution
- Domain Evolution
- Continuous Improvement
- Architectural Consistency

This capability is **not** responsible for:

- Learning
- Knowledge Management
- Assessment
- Day-to-day study execution

---

# Architecture State

Architecture Governance & Evolution owns:

- Architecture Principles
- ADRs
- Design Journal
- Capability Model
- Domain Models
- Enterprise Models

---

# Inputs

- Implementation Feedback
- Learning Feedback
- Assessment Feedback
- Architectural Reviews
- New Ideas
- Emerging Requirements

---

# Outputs

- Updated ADRs
- New Principles
- Revised Models
- Architecture Improvements
- Future Roadmap

---

# Architectural Principles

## Evolution Over Perfection

Architecture should evolve through implementation feedback.

---

## Architecture Before Implementation

Capabilities and models precede workflows and tools.

---

## Traceable Decisions

Every significant architectural decision should be recorded through an ADR.

---

## Continuous Governance

Governance is an ongoing capability rather than a one-time activity.

---

# Collaboration

Architecture Governance & Evolution collaborates with all enterprise capabilities.

---

# Architecture Principle

> **Architecture is a living system that evolves through disciplined learning rather than isolated design decisions.**
