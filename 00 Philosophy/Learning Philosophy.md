# Learning Philosophy

## North Star
Good architecture makes the right thing easy and the wrong thing difficult.

## Principles
- Purpose drives behaviour.
- Relationships matter more than components.
- Stocks are more important than effort.
- Feedback drives adaptation.
- Optimize bottlenecks.
- Respect delays.
- Emergence through interconnected knowledge.
- Anti-fragility.
- Measure learning, not activity.
- Self-correcting systems.
